Synopsis

In every mind exists an “Ocean Door” — a hidden gateway to the subconscious world, and only two telepaths can control it. Kallista, a young telepath with no memory of her early life, awakens in the secluded Vale and has been trained by the mysterious Vow for years. Determined to escape, she faces pipe‑smoking spies who vanish from memory, a red‑haired ghost in the west woods, and a strange boy with uncanny powers. To break free, she must master the peculiar ability to infiltrate the subconscious through a blue door and unravel the Vale’s deepest secrets.
